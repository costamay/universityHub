About UniHub

Unihub was created and designed by a group of developers and designers. This was after each gave their testimony of how hard it is to get constructive feedback on your project.

And the reason its hard is mostly because, we tend to ask for feedback from those people who are our friends and close to us since they are our connections.

But, most of the time, and most of them dont give us the right feedback because they fear discouraging us Because at times they Know how much we have invested our time in those projects.

Another problem is that, most will give us feedback which is not relevant. While others will give a feedback that you dont know how to fix it but its very crucial to the project.

Thats, where now UniHub comes in. We want to make your process to be less hectic. When you through with your project, We have our projects page, post your pojects, then we shall give you consteuctive feedback and even have time that will guide you on how to implement that feedback.
